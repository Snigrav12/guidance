Eligibility Criteria and Admission Process

At Harvard, we seek students who demonstrate exceptional academic ability and show significant promise for success at Harvard and beyond. To apply, you need to fulfill the following criteria:

  • Completion of secondary education
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal essay
  • Extracurricular achievements

Our admission process is holistic and considers various aspects of a candidate's profile.

Application Process

To apply to Harvard College, follow these steps:

  1. Submit the Common Application or Coalition Application
  2. Complete the Harvard Questions and Writing Supplement
  3. Pay the application fee or request a fee waiver
  4. Submit your standardized test scores
  5. Submit two letters of recommendation from teachers
  6. Submit a mid-year school report
