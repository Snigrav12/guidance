Collage Preview

S Arts College focuses primarily on the development of the academic excellence and wide spectrum towards student enhancement. The holistic enrichment of a student’s persona is the core objective of the college. As the college is situated at the prime location of Jaipur city, it is easily accessible to all. The college has a well-developed infrastructure with a playground, smart classes and every other essential amenity. Two well furnished Conference halls & more than 33 well ventilated classrooms form a part of the modern infrastructure of the college. It has well stacked library with more than 22148 books. It is well equipped with internet facility for the students. The college has a history of being associated with profound educators, under whose guidance the college has touched the epitome of brilliance. The college has techno-friendly approach in the pursuit of education. In an increasingly technique driven world, the college facilitates the technical growth of the students. Online class at the time of pandemic illustrates the use of such techniques. Video-Meet, Zoom, Google Meet were some of the online platforms used by the college at pandemic. This depicts college’s scholastic and technological approach. Keeping in mind the need of the hour, the college oﬀers a full range of support and career counseling services to the students. More than 3600 students every year have been imparted quality education at the college, which is a landmark. The college keeps up its tradition of brilliance and commitment in the field of higher education. While we believe in the importance of practical knowledge, we also firmly believe in a complete & rounded education. It has several provisions for the skilled personality development to groom the students who come from rural background. The college time to time organizes seminars, debates, quizzes, ICT enabled classes, and other academic programs to make learning interested. Extracurricular activities to facilitate overall growth of students are one of the prime concerns of the college. The college grants scholarships and other financial aids to meritorious students and students coming from weak economical background.
